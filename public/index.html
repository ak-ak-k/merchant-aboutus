About REFR

Refr is on a mission to democratize commerce. Since our inception, we’ve relentlessly focused on creating new frontiers in online and retail commerce, where the customers shopping experience feels more connected and informed , as for the Merchants we provide an easy and effective marketing and selling platform .

We aim grow into a network of tens of millions of shoppers ready for product and service discovery at point of inspiration on social and chat via a peer-peer recommendation led model. Refr is levelling the playing field for retailers and making shopping easy, trusted, and secure for consumers, thus creating a truly open easy and inclusive ecosystem that benefits all.
